About Me

Albert Sroder
I am a UI Artist, UI Designer and a UX Designer. I began my career by making advertising websites and banners, but searched for any opportunity to get into the gaming Industry. After I found a way in, I started by working on simple web games until I got the opportunity to work on mobile games. Soon I became the main artist on a 6-month long mobile game project which currently has over 1 million downloads. Then, life took a turn, I moved to the UK and I have been working on a mobile AR social game and other small VR projects.
